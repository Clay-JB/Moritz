Illustrations


Most of my illustrations were made using Paint Tool Sai, Krita and Procreate, although I’m also well versed in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and a host of more niche programs. I’ve been doing digital art for about a decade and am always looking to improve.

Audio

I enjoyed an extensive musical education before university, and have since taken to composing, recording and mixing. I’ve recorded instruments and vocals for music, worked with synthesisers and samplers in Logic Pro X, I’ve been in charge of voice acting direction in a project with several hundred lines spread across about 10 voiceactors (myself included), recorded Foley, I’ve worked with FMOD for adaptive soundtrack and ambience, and cut, edited and mixed music, sfx, ui noises and ambience for games.

Music

Bossfight theme of Rhythm of Triverz. The game features a rhythm element, so the entire song post intro is paced evenly. The rhythm signature changes, but the tempo sticks to 120 bpm. There are several stages of intensity to this song, in game they are dependent on the player's skill.
